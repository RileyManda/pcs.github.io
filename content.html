Mission

Credit Repair Services

A poor Credit score and challenged credit history can be expensive for you but the good news is that you are not alone and plenty of people struggle to correct their credit history and improve their credit score as well as chances of loan acceptance. This is where PCS can help.

Credit Profile Analysis and Problem identification

On receipt of your Detect Report, our operation team will have a one to one talk with you and discuss in detail the issues on your credit report and also take information from you on the loan products you have serviced. After this process, our operation team will update you on errors if any on your credit report and also update you on the issues on your credit report that are affecting your credit score and in turn your ability to avail any credit facilities with financial institutions.

What do we do?

PCS takes problematic credit reports seriously. We understand the applicable consumer protection and privacy laws, and we help our clients to leverage and grip those legal rights so that their credit reports remain fair, accurate, and substantiated. From bankruptcies to charge-offs, to tax liens, we have challenged virtually every credit problem, doing work for our clients that resulted in thousands of items removed from their credit reports last year alone. Our 7+ years of experience and counting more in the credit repair field have helped many clients to turn their lives around. Credit Repair is not what many people believe it is. Effective, enduring credit repair requires taking appropriate and correct action with your creditors rather than simply creating ineffective disputes for credit bureaus. We help our clients frame the tough questions that they are legally entitled to ask, and we invoke several consumer protection statutes in our work to improve their lives.
