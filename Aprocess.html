Case Intake

Once you engage in our firm, we help you obtain your credit reports from the credit bureau: CIBIL. Since each client's case is unique and important for us, we will collect specific information regarding your particular circumstances for each item in question.

Legal Action

The Credit Analyst assigned to your case will review and evaluate your credit reports and then direct report an approach it to your creditors and the credit bureaus. Your credit report items are carefully diagnosed and matched with just the right credit repair strategies and best plans as applicable to your case.

Credit Score Analysis

Our clients receive a personalized credit score improvement analysis, including targeted information that may help to raise and uplift their credit scores.

What bad credit costs?

Credit is a necessity. Whether it’s a mortgage, a car loan, or a credit card, good and fine credit creates opportunities. Bad credit doesn’t. With today's continuous rise in economic challenges, it is essential to have a healthy credit score. For those with bad credit Score, increased interest rates may further inflate their cost of living, making even small purchases seem too difficult. A bad credit report can cost you thousands of rupees a year in overpaid interest or can keep you from being approved for credit altogether. It is easy to see the financial burden created by a bad credit score, but the real cost is more difficult to measure. Bad credit affects more than your finances, it can also attack your sense of security, and limit your freedom. Working with Prince Credit Service to take action on your credit, is much easier to quantify.
