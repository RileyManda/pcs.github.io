CIBIL - Credit Information Report for Consumers

A credit information report plays an essential role in a lender’s decision when applying for credit, so it's extremely necessary to monitor it regularly to check for any inaccuracies. The Credit Information Report is a collection of your credit history. The data in the CIBIL, Credit Information Report is based on the information provided to us by all our member banks, financial institutions, and other credit grantors.

What particulars does your (Cibil) Credit Information Report cover?

Your Credit Information Report contains all the information regarding your credit accounts and details of any credit applications you have made, which consists of all the public and private information recorded in your name and available to lenders carrying out a credit check through civil.

What are the advantages of obtaining a copy of your Credit Information Report?

As your credit history plays an essential role in your ability to obtain credit and on what terms, it is important to understand the information that is shared by lenders with a credit information company, such as Cibil. Understanding and exploring your credit history enables you to take control of your financial situation, make informed financial and accurate decisions, and also helps to protect yourself from identity theft.

HOW MUCH WILL YOU INCREASE MY CREDIT SCORE?

Our average is over 50 points within three months, although we have been as successful as 200 points. The exact amount we can raise your score is dependent on your current situation and your ability to follow the advice we give.

WHAT ITEMS CAN YOU REMOVE OR DETACH FROM MY CREDIT REPORT?

Wrong balances & overdue or delayed amounts, wilful default, suit filed, charge off, written off, settled & unsettled a/c’s, collections, multiple identities, inaccurate accounts, identity theft, and more.

WHAT ITEMS CAN’T YOU REMOVE?

Anything which is 100% accurate and verifiable our average removal rate is 90%.

WILL THE REMOVED ITEMS EVER COME BACK?

No, as long as the item is correct and accurate or paid at the time of removal or it is a collection account older than two years.

WILL 100% OF MY NEGATIVE ITEMS BE REMOVED?

We have been 100% successful on many occasions. Our average success rate is around 90%.

WHY DO RESULTS DIFFER?

We use and apply consumer protection laws to their fullest on your behalf. Sometimes credit bureaus, creditors, and collection agencies provide all the law requires, and sometimes they don’t. We are completely thorough in enforcing your rights and can find a reason to remove a negative item from your credit report an average of 70% of the time.

WHAT DO I DO TO GET STARTED?

Simply give us a call to go over and review your credit report so that we can create an action plan for you.
